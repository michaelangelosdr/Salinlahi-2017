Dr. Barba is best known for inventing a way to induce more flowers in mango trees using ethrel and potassium nitrate./
Dr. Barba was also recognized as one of the Ten Outstanding Young Men in Agriculture in 1974, and was given the Horticultural Technology Award in June 1999./
Philippines is now the world's second biggest mango exporter (7.8% share) behind Mexico (41%) whose main variety "Manila" and its KNO3 flowering technology are both of Philippine origin./
